Project description

Our work consists on analyzing grants and subsidies from the Generalitat Valenciana, employment contracts by municipality, gender, age, and sector, and temporary employment regulation files (ERTE). Trends in aid distribution, employment evolution, and the most in-demand occupations are compared, allowing assessment of their impact on different sectors and populations.
